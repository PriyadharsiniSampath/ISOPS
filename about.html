Our Story

ISOPS was founded in 2012 by a group of changemakers committed to rethinking humanitarian and social development through innovation, inclusion, and shared power. Officially registered in Nigeria in 2015, ISOPS has evolved into a growing global network that connects communities, government, and private sectors to co-create sustainable progress.

Our Vision

A world where smart, inclusive systems empower people to create lasting change.

Our Mission

To prototype solutions that promote equality, sustainability, and progress through partnerships between communities, government, and private sectors.
